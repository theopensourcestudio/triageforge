# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone. Be constructive, discuss the work rather than the person, respect privacy, and do not pressure maintainers for unpaid labour.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, threats, discriminatory language, publishing private information, sexualised attention, and sustained disruption.

Maintainers may edit, remove, reject, lock, or ban contributions that violate these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner's published contact address. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

This policy is adapted in spirit from widely used open-source community standards and intentionally written in plain language.
